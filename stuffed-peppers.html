Stuffed Peppers

Description

These Stuffed Bell Peppers are an easy and healthy dinner recipe that you can make with a your choice of filling options, including ground beef, cheese, mushrooms, tomato sauce, and more! Your taste buds imagination have no limits!

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef and Sausage(or any other meat you'd like)
  • Garlic
  • Minced Onion
  • Diced Tomatoes
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Rice
  • Cheddar Cheese
  • 6 Green, Red or Yellow Peppers

Steps

  1. Slice the top off of the peppers and remove the seeds/membranes. Place them top-side-down in a casserole dish with water. Cover and steam them for 20 minutes. Remove water and place the peppers back in the casserole dish. While the peppers steam, prepare the filling.
  2. Prepare the meat/sauce/rice mixture and fill the middle of each bell pepper. Cover and bake for 20 minutes.
  3. Remove cover and top with cheese. Bake uncovered for 10 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and the peppers are tender. Remove and serve!
Side Notes
  1. Some recipes call for boiling, blanching, or steaming bell peppers before stuffing them, while others leave the peppers raw. No matter what, the peppers require at least 50 minutes of cooking time to have the ideal finished texture.
